KWS Middle East - Business Setup Consultant Dubai

Your Guide to Successful Business Setup in UAE

Setting up a new business in UAE can be a complicated, time consuming and intimidating process without the right support and services of an experienced consultancy provider.

This is where KWSME comes to your rescue. Established in 2010. We are a team of professional business setup consultants based in Dubai, UAE. We offer comprehensive company formation services to all aspiring entrepreneurs, SMEs and international firms looking to setup their business in Dubai.

Our company registration experts remove the complexity of company formation in UAE so that you can focus on your core business while we build a good foundation for your company. You have our support in each step of the way whether you are looking for company formation in Dubai Mainland, Free Zones or to set up an Offshore Company. Along with providing company registration services, we also offer PRO services, legal translation, visa, creation of bank account, company liquidation, trademark registration as well as office space in UAE.

At KWSME, we work hard to make sure our clients’ needs are completely met with so as to achieve customer satisfaction. Our experts go through company incorporation process in such a seamless manner so that your company becomes a winning enterprise in UAE.
